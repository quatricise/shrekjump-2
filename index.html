Tips & Tricks
Each destroyed platform will hamper the game scroll speed, the more you destroy, the slower you go crazy.
Collecting most pickups will increase your score.
Press the Down arrow key to fall faster. You can also hold it while jumping to instantly begin quick-falling once your jump is over.
Losing hearts intentionally can increase your score as it leads to spawning more hearts. It makes the game more risky however, so be careful.
Watch out for golden hearts, they permanently increase your heart capacity.
Press [E] to eat, if you have collected any onions. The onion will temporarily hyper-charge your stamina gain, use it at the right moment.
Press [Left Shift] to dash. Dashing gives you momentary velocity boost in the direction you're facing. But don't forget you have to look left or right, otherwise the dash won't work.
If you're glowing blue, it means your dash is ready.
Jumping several times on a platform will release the spike, giving you extra score.
Sometimes shrek will glitch and fall through a platform. This is not intentional, I'm very sorry.
